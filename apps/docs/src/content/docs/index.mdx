## What is AlbertPlus?

AlbertPlus is a powerful, open-source platform designed to enhance the course registration experience for New York University (NYU) students. It provides a modern, intuitive interface and a suite of tools to help students plan their academic journey, build schedules, and navigate the complexities of course selection. This project is developed and maintained by the vibrant community at Tech@NYU.

## Key Features

- **Modern Web Interface**: A fast, responsive, and user-friendly web application built with Next.js and React.
- **Browser Extension**: A companion Chrome extension that integrates directly with the Albert website.
- **Real-time Course Data**: An automated scraper keeps course information up-to-date.
- **Degree Progress Tracking**: Upload and parse your degree progress report to see which requirements you've fulfilled.
- **Schedule Builder**: Visually construct and manage your course schedule with a drag-and-drop calendar interface.
- **Comprehensive Backend**: A robust serverless backend powered by Convex, providing real-time data synchronization.

## Getting Involved

Whether you're a student looking to improve your registration experience or a developer eager to contribute to a meaningful project, AlbertPlus welcomes you. Explore our documentation to get started, and join our community on Discord to connect with other contributors.
